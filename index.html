Jiyeon Lee is a second-year Master's student and Harvard-Yenching Fellow in Regional Studies - East Asia (RSEA) Program at Harvard University.

Her research interests broadly concern social boundaries and belonging-making processes, as well as how these are interactively negotiated and relationally maintained by individual and institutional actors. Her recent work explores the gendered processes of immigrant integration in the neoliberal welfare state.
